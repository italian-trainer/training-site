Alcohol Awareness

As a server for Maria’s Italian Kitchen, alcohol service will be a big part of your job. It’s a part of your job that comes with a lot of accountability.

As a server for Maria’s Italian Kitchen, YOU are responsible for the safe and appropriate service of alcoholic beverages within the law.

Responsible alcohol service impacts the safety of our guests and neighbors, and the liability of our restaurants and team members. When a drunk driver is involved in an driver was served liable for the damage caused, and that liability extends to the server or bartender who brought them the drinks.

As a step toward protecting against the likelihood of “over service”, restaurants like Maria’s have developed policies and practices for monitoring the service of alcohol. While management is there for 100% support on this issue when you need them, as the server, you will be the person in the best position to make critical judgments about whether or not it is safe to continue serving a guest.
