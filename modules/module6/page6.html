Server: Sequence of Service Part 2

13. Check Back

Checking back shortly after the main course is delivered is important to ensure that guests have everything they need and that their orders are to their liking.

  • Check back should happen within two minutes or two bites. That leaves enough time for the guest to get into what they have and determine if there is anything they need, without leaving them waiting too long if things aren’t right.
  • Your check back should always be specific.
    • “How is your Chicken Parmigiana?”
    • “Are we enjoying the pizza?”
    • This will help to personalize your interactions.
    • Generic questions only elicit generic responses and we really want to know how the guest is enjoying their experience.
  • This is a good time to also do some table maintenance and double-check beverages for refills.
