7. Prepare Table for First Course

Before the first course arrives you should do a little table maintenance and double- check the table to make sure they have everything they need. The goal is to ensure that the guest always has everything they need before they need it.

  • Missing silverware
  • Napkins
  • Drink refills
  • Pizza Stands
  • Etc.
